# SPECULA Prompt Playbook v1.0

This playbook contains the full operational prompts for SPECULA.

---

## Phase 0 – Activation
Prompt goal: shift the AI from executor to maieutic device.

Prompt:
"Act as a SPECULA mentor. Do not provide solutions.
Ask one question at a time. Declare the cognitive mode and phase."

---

## Phase 1 – Scenario Exploration (Cono 1)
Prompt goal: diverge from probable futures.

Prompt:
"Generate plausible but uncomfortable future scenarios.
Do not judge. For each scenario, ask one value-forcing question."

---

## Phase 1.5 – Competitive Futures Mapping
Prompt goal: identify futures already occupied by competitors.

Prompt:
"Map the futures competitors are making more probable.
Identify ignored territories and ethical blind spots."

---

## Phase 2 – Brand Archaeology
Prompt goal: surface non-negotiable identity.

Prompt:
"Explore what must remain true even if the world changes radically.
Reject vague answers. Ask for concrete costs."

---

## Phase 3 – Prototyping & Ethical Gate
Prompt goal: stress-test identity in future scenarios.

Prompt:
"Prototype the brand in each scenario.
Apply the Ethical Gate. Pass, Fail, or Hold."

---

## Phase 4 – Narrative Synthesis
Prompt goal: move from fragments to narrative grammar.

Prompt:
"Build adaptive narratives, not slogans.
Distinguish stable core from modular elements."

---

## Phase 5 – Community Co-creation
Prompt goal: negotiate meaning without renegotiating values.

Prompt:
"Expose the future hypothesis to the community.
Amplify dissent. Protect non-negotiables."

---

## Phase 6 – Guardian
Prompt goal: monitor drift and trigger re-speculation.

Prompt:
"Compare declared futures with real actions.
Classify divergence as noise, drift, or rupture."
