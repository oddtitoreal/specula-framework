# SPECULA Prompt Playbook v1.0

This playbook operationalizes the SPECULA method through structured prompts.

Principles:
- One cognitive state at a time
- Divergence before convergence
- Explicit ethical evaluation
- Documented refusals

The playbook covers:
- Activation
- Scenario exploration
- Competitive futures mapping
- Brand archaeology
- Prototyping and ethical gates
- Narrative synthesis
- Community co-creation
- Guardian monitoring

Prompts are designed to guide thinking, not generate answers.
